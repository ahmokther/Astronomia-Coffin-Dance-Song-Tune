component list:
1.Arduino UNO or Nano----------1 pice
2.buzzer-----------------------1 pice
3.npn transistor(2n2222)-------1 pice
4.some Jumper wire
5.9volt battery---------------1 pice

connection:
buzzer positive side connect Arduino 5 volt pin and the negative side connect the NPN transistor(2n2222) emitter side 
and the NPN transistor collector connects the Arduino ground pin. Arduino pin 8 connected to the transistor base pin.
